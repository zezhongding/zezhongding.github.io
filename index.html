    Research Interests

    My general research interests mainly focus on the areas of data science, system, and artificial intelligence over big data, particularly big graph data. The current structure of my research framework is as follows.

    • Data Science [Data Management Layer]
    • 🎯Graph Partitioning [S5P, SIGMOD-24]
    • 🎯Structural Graph Clustering
    • System [System Architecture Layer]
    • 🎯Graph Learning System
    • Artificial Intelligence [Downstream Task Layer]
    • 🎯Graph Sampling for GNN
    • 🎯Attribute Graph Clustering with GNN
    • 🎯Disease Inference with Knowledge Graphs (Cooperation with iFLYTEK)
